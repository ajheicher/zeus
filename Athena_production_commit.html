    • Inclement Weather Staff Policy

      • In order to avoid disruption of service to our customers, it is our intent to remain open except in instances of extreme weather (state of emergency). At times of extreme weather, if a decision is made to open late or close for business, we will have a message on our inclement weather hotline (215-790-5895) after 7:00am. Should you call and receive our regular greeting, it means we are open for business as usual.

      We do not want staff to take unwarranted risks traveling to and from work and ask that you exercise your best judgment with regard to road conditions and personal safety concerns. If you are unable to report to work, please notify your supervisor. Absences or lateness due to inclement weather may be charged to personal days, floating holidays or vacation. If you have any questions, please contact your supervisor or Human Resources for clarification.

      Also, please read your email for important updates from Michelle Harris about specific performances.

    KimmelSTIX - Student Ticket Program

    Click HERE for KimmelSTIX email!

    Kimmel STIX allows students to access $10 tickets to select KCP & BWY performances in advance!

    Unlike eZseatU, the Kimmel STIX program assigns a student a seat during their purchase! There are no additional fees.

    Kimmel STIX is an online only ticket program. Students pre-register to receive emails from the Kimmel. When tickets are available, an email is sent with a promo code to purchase tickets. Tickets are held at will-call and student IDs are checked at the box office when the tickets are picked up. One ticket per student. Students need to pick up their tickets no later than 30 minutes prior to performance time.

    TROUBLESHOOTING:
    Remember – KimmelSTIX tickets are LIMITED AVAILABILITY. If a student is having issues online, the allocation of tickets is probably sold out. The promo code also needs to be entered!

    Some more details:

    What is KimmelSTIX?
    A members-only connection for $10 student tickets to select Kimmel Center and Broadway performances. Members receive periodic email notifications about $10 ticket offers. Buy tickets online with your KimmelSTIX promo code, pay NO service fees and get the best available seating!

    Who can join?
    KimmelSTIX is open to high school, college and graduate students, ages 17 to 30, with a valid student I.D. card.

    How do I join?
    Fill out the online registration form. Once you’ve registered, you’ll receive monthly emails with the latest $10 ticket offers.

    Online form can be found here:

    http://www.kimmelcenter.org/events/kimmelstix.php - weblink

    Multiple Charges & Other Credit Card Questions

    A lien is temporarily item on a patron’s account - debit or credit - that appears as a charge and holds money for a transaction before it is fully processed. Whenever a card is authorized in Tessitura or online, even if it does not go through, it can create a lien. Liens can last 24 hours to 7 business days. This can be especially problematic on a debit card, because it prevents the funds from being accessed.


    What is the best way to handle this with our patrons?
    If you receive a call that a patron is showing multiple charges on their card from either purchasing tickets online or over the phone, first check their account in Tessitura to ensure that the order only went through one time. If there are multiple orders in the account, speak to a lead about canceling the order the patron does not want, and issue a credit to the patron. Credits can take up to 2-3 business days to reflect on a patron’s account.

    If there is only one transaction listed in tessitura, explain to the patron that this transaction only went through one time (verify the amount), and all other charges will drop off of their card in up to 7 business days.

    If there is a patron complaining about a lien they want dropped please explained to them that a lien typically falls away within 3-5 business days. We can fax documentation to the bank to request that they remove the lien more quickly. However, even when we fax documentation it still typically takes the bank 1-2 days to remove the lien.

    If they insists on having the charges dropped immediately, please send the following information to LINDSAY (lkrieg@ticketphiladelphia.org):
    • Last four digits of CC number that has the lien and the card’s expiration date
    • Time and Date in which the order was attempted
    • The mode in which the order was processed (TP, PBA, POA, web/phone)
    • Total amount and # of charges they are seeing (3 charges of $125.00)
    • Issuing bank information (issuing bank name and where the bank would like us to send the documentation – can be a fax # or phone #). If the patron does not know, please advise them they should call their bank and find out. A bank will typically not talk to someone who is not an account holder there, so it is vital the patron provide us with this information.
